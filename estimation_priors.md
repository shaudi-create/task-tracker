# Estimation Priors

These are the user's typical durations for recurring work patterns. Use as a calibrated prior; deviate when task details suggest otherwise.

## Meetings & comms
- 1:1 meeting: 30 min
- Team standup: 15 min
- Client meeting (kickoff or status): 60 min
- Internal sync with 4+ people: 45 min
- Slack triage / inbox zero pass: 20 min

## Writing
- Email reply (substantive): 10 min
- Slide prep for standup: 30 min
- Slide prep for stakeholder review (10-20 slides): 90 min
- Memo draft (500–800 words): 60 min
- Blog post draft (~1000 words): 90 min

## Coding / technical
- PR review (small, <200 LOC): 20 min
- PR review (medium, 200–800 LOC): 45 min
- Debug an unfamiliar issue: 60 min (very variable)
- Write unit tests for a function: 30 min

## Errands & life
- Grocery run in Williamsburg: 30 min
- Doctor / dentist appointment: 60 min (incl. waiting)
- Lunch out: 60 min
- Workout: 45 min
- Pick up medication from CVS Pharmacy (Williamsburg): 20 min
- Pick up mail from front desk at The Edge: 10 min
- Pilates Reformer class: 75 min

## Home & family
- Light weekly cleaning of apartment: 60 min
- Deep clean apartment: 180 min
- IT debugging session for Mom and Dad: 75 min

## Deep work
- Focused writing block: 90 min
- Strategy / planning session (solo): 60 min
